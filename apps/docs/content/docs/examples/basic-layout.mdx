## When to use

Use this for cards that are cheap to render and do not need shortest-column
balancing. It is the best default for product tiles, settings panels, and
simple content collections.

## Notes

- `columnWidth` keeps cards readable as the container grows.
- `maxColumns` prevents dense, hard-to-scan layouts on wide screens.
- `itemKey` keeps identity stable during filters and sorting.
